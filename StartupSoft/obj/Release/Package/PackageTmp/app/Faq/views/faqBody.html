FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Do you charge an hourly rate?
We charge the bare minimum to simply pay our bills. At only $8 per hour, you will receive a package of software design and development work, as well as marketing and business consulting.
2. How much equity do you take?
We typically accept between 5-25%.
3. Who is on your team?
Our team consists of several dozen members and includes developers, graphic designers, testers, marketers and advisors.
4. Where are you based?
StartupSoft Inc. is a Canadian corporation with a presence in Toronto and Europe.
5. How can I know if my application has been accepted?
Once your application has been reviewed, we will notify you of our decision. We accept 5% of applicants on average and it may take several business days for us to review your application.
